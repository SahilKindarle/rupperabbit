Ethic's Privacy Policy

MAY 22, 2018
Ethic’s number one priority is your trust. Your privacy is essential to earning and keeping that trust. This tenet drives all the decisions we make, as well as how we gather, use and store any information we acquire from you.We created this Privacy Policy to be as clear and direct as possible about how we gather and use your information. To ensure your privacy we store your personal data (“Personal Data”) on our secure cloud-based servers. The reason we collect Personal Data is to ensure we are in a position to best serve you. We will never rent, sell or trade your personal information to anyone. Ever.
1. Introduction
Ethic Inc. (“Ethic”, “we”, “us” and/or “our”) operates an automated investment service (“Service”). Our Service is made available through our websites (www.ethic.tech and www.ethic.investments). This Privacy Policy describes how Ethic treats your Personal Data when you use or evaluate our service.For the purpose of this policy a User is an entity or an individual who uses our website to evaluate our service, or for educational purposes, and a Client is a company or an individual who signs an agreement with us that entitles the Client to have an investment portfolio managed or advised by Ethic. Our Privacy Policy, Terms of Use and where applicable Client Agreement collectively govern your use or evaluation of our service.
2. Access to the site
The types of information we collect depend on whether you are a User or Client. We collect Personal Data from you when you voluntarily provide information to us. Examples of instances when we collect Personal Data include when you answer questions on our website to determine what kind of portfolio we might recommend if you were to become a Client, when you register to open an account, when you contact our client service organization with questions or when you become a Client. Wherever Ethic collects Personal Data, we make an effort to link to this Privacy Policy and other relevant terms, such as our Terms of Use.
2.1 In general
Ethic’s servers automatically record certain information (“Non-Identifiable” or “Aggregated Data”) about your use or evaluation of our Service. Similar to other technology platforms and services, Ethic records information such as browsing activity, data displayed or clicked on (such as UI elements, and links), and other information (such as browser type, IP address, date and time of access, cookie ID, and referrer URL). Along with cookies, Ethic may also use third-party tracking technology, such as Google Analytics to record similar information regarding you and your activity on the Site.
2.2 Information collection from users
Information Collection from Users. We collect information such as name, telephone number and other optional information you may provide, which may include your financial and sustainability goals and objectives, and other financial information. We may collect your e-mail address and/or telephone number should you opt to engage via our website or email.
2.3 Information collection from clients
Information Collection from Clients. If you choose to become a Client, in addition to the information we collect from you as a User, as described above, we will ask you for additional information during the onboarding process for a Client. This additional information will differ depending on whether the Client is an individual or an entity.
2.4 Acting as your authorized agent
Ethic offers services where Ethic acts as an agent to retrieve your financial account information, such as your account balances and holdings, from financial institutions designated. By accessing and using the Ethic services you expressly authorize and direct Ethic, on your behalf, to electronically retrieve your account information maintained by third-party financial institutions with which you have a legally binding customer relationship (“Account Information”). Ethic may work with one or more third-party financial service technology providers to access and retrieve your Account Information.

Ethic is not responsible for and cannot guarantee the accuracy or timeliness of Account Information we retrieve on your behalf directly, from third-party technology providers, from third-party financial institutions where your accounts are held, or from third-party market data providers used to provide the Services.
3. Use of information
Ethic stores, processes, and maintains data related to you in order to provide client support, offer new products or services and provide our Service to Clients in accordance with the rules of regulatory bodies such as the Securities and Exchange Commission and FINRA. You may choose not to provide such information to us, but if you choose not to provide such information, you will not be able to become a Client.We may share the information required to become a Client with select brokerage partners solely for the purpose of allowing our brokerage partner to provide services to you.Ethic may use your Personal Data to communicate with you regarding our Service or to tell you about blog posts or services that we believe will be of interest to you. If you decide at any time that you no longer wish to receive marketing communications from us, please follow the “unsubscribe” instructions provided in the communications. Please note that you cannot opt-out of administrative communications such as regulatory, billing or service notifications.
4. Information sharing and onward transfer
We will not sell, rent, or trade your Personal Data with any third parties except as required by law, such as when we reasonably believe it is necessary or appropriate to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, or violations of our Terms of Use.There are certain circumstances, outlined below, in which we may share your Personal Data without further disclosure to you. From time-to-time, we hire other companies to perform certain business and technology related functions (e.g. mailing information, database maintenance and payment processing). In the case we hire another company to perform a function of this kind, we only provide them with the minimum information they need to perform their specific function. Ethic maintains strict procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to guard your Non-Public personal information.Except as required by law or requested by regulatory authorities, Ethic agrees to maintain your non-public Personal Data in strict confidence.
5. Your choices and obligations
You may request to update, access or delete any or all of your personal information by emailing support@ethicinvesting.com and providing us enough information to identify your account and prove that you are the owner of the identified account. We will endeavor to make any update, access or delete request effective as soon as reasonably practicable. However, we may retain residual information related to your account, as well as any data related to your trades, in accordance with applicable laws. In no case will we share any individual trading data unless required by regulators or other government bodies.You may decline to provide personal information to Ethic. Declining to provide personal information may disqualify you for Ethic services and Site features that require certain personal information.
